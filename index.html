Plant Growth Process

Start: The process begins with planting a seed. Plant Seed: The seed is placed into the soil. Water Seed: The seed needs water to start growing. Is Soil Fertile?: A decision point checking if the soil has nutrients.

If No, soil is improved (e.g., adding compost or fertilizer), then watering continues. If Yes, the seed continues to germinate. Seed Germinates: The seed sprouts roots and shoots. Seedling Grows: A small plant (seedling) starts to grow. Mature Plant: The seedling becomes a fully grown plant. End: The growth process completes.
